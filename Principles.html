Principles

Web accessibility

Web accessibility is the practice of making websites accessible to people with disabilities. This includes people who are blind or have low vision, deaf or hard of hearing, have learning disabilities, or have motor impairments.

Web browser compatibility

Web browser compatibility is the practice of ensuring that websites work correctly in different web browsers. This includes different versions of the same browser, as well as different browsers on different platforms.
